# Pattern

Renders the simplified graphic pattern in a range of colour palettes, as defined in The Vanguard Brand Guidelines<sup><a href="#the-vanguard-brand-guidelines">1</a></sup>.  
Consisting of a pair of two colour tones with minimal graphic forms which have been created from the curves and angles from our V closely cropped. It can be used with a background colour, or without to allow images or other content to still be visible.

## Colour & Positioning

The Pattern can be styled in 4 colour palettes, which should not be mixed with other palettes on the same page. They are available through the `palette` attribute:

- Vanguard Red
- Dark Red
- Turquoise
- Yellow

The Pattern can also be positioned in 3 different orientations, which is can be changed by the `position` attribute:

- Left
- Center
- Right

When positioned `right` the Pattern should intersect with the bottom right corner.

## Combining patterns with additional elements

We do not combine our patterns with other visual elements like icons, illustrations, or data visualisations. In specific use cases, we may use our patterns with photography, such as in the [Banner Hero](?path=/docs/organisms-banners-banner-hero).

<div class="grid-col-2">
<Do img={patternCombiningDo}>

**Do** use with concise messaging, logos and simple UI elements like buttons or links.

</Do>
<DoNot img={patternCombiningDoNot}>

**Do not** combine with large amound of text or other detailed visual elements like data visualisations or illustrations.

</DoNot>
</div>
